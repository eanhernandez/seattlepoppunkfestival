SICKO FRONTMAN EAN HERNANDEZ ANNOUNCES '14 Soda Punx' COMPILATION LP + SEATTLE POP PUNK FESTIVAL 2018 In 1993, Sicko frontman Ean Hernandez' label, Top Drawer Records released '13 Soda Punx', with Fastbacks, Sicko, Huevos Rancheros, Old Man, Model Rockets, Mr T Experience, Stand GT, The Stuppes, Young Fresh Fellows, The Primate 5, Bum, Cub, and The Smugglers.  Now 23 years later, we have the 2nd edition: '14 Soda Punx', with a mountain of Seattle based Pop Punk bands.  This is a documentation of a thriving underground scene. We've been playing this music since the eighties, and we never stopped!  This time around we've got Success, Ramona, Listen Lady, Choke the Pope, Foxhole Norman, Coyote Bred, Four Lights, Shadow Cats, Dead Bars, Botherations, Stuporhero, Burn Burn Burn, The Drolls, and Date Night with Brian.  All Seattle, all Pop Punk, and all KILLR.  To celebrate the release of the LP, Top Drawer Records announced Seattle Pop Punk Festival in January of 2018.
